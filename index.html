Hangman!

Description:

Hangman has been around for a long time. A word, phase, or sentence is hidden and you try and guess the word by selecting letters, within a certain number of guesses. In this game we will be using names! More specifically Video Game Characters!
You will have a total of SIX wrong answers, when you hit that limit you lose and the game will end. You will then need to refresh the page to start over with a new word.
