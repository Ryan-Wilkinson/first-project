Hangman!

Description:

Hangman has been around for a long time. A word, phase, or sentence is hidden and you try and guess the word by selecting letters, within a certain number of guesses. In this game we will be using names! More specifically Overwatch Heroes! You will have a total of SIX wrong answers, when you hit that limit you lose and the game will end. After you win or lose the game will auto refresh after FIVE SECONDS. Just kick back and let the games keep rolling!
